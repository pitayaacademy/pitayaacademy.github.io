Portfolio-first learning

Build real projects with modern AI

A guided path for high school students to learn practical AI skills and publish meaningful projects using authentic datasets from science and health domains. The result: a portfolio that shows initiative, skill, and impact.

What students learn

  • Data skills: sourcing, cleaning, and responsible use
  • Modeling: from baselines to modern techniques
  • Communication: explain methods and results clearly

How we work

  • Weekly mentorship and checkpoints
  • Project templates and code reviews
  • Publishing to GitHub Pages and simple reports

Typical projects

  • Exploratory dashboards and visual stories
  • Structured prediction with careful evaluation
  • Ethics and safety considerations in practice

Outcomes

  • Published portfolio pieces with write-ups and code
  • Confidence using modern AI tooling responsibly
  • Strong talking points for applications and interviews

Who it's for

Motivated high school students who enjoy hands-on learning and want to build things that matter, step by step.
