LEGO Robotics

Coaching for FLL Explore and FLL Challenge

We focus on growth, teamwork, and purposeful iteration while making robotics fun and approachable for every student.

FLL Explore Teams

Ages 6–10 • 6–10 students per team

  • Hands-on building with guided challenges
  • Introduce motors, sensors, and simple programming concepts
  • Practice sharing ideas and presenting work

Season Fall–Winter • Showcase Expo presentations

What students gain

  • Curiosity and confidence
  • Teamwork and communication
  • Creative problem solving

FLL Challenge Teams

Ages 9–14 • 6–8 students per team

  • Robot design, coding, mission strategy
  • Iterative testing and match practice
  • Innovation project research and presentation

Season Summer–Winter • Events Qualifiers + Invitationals

Coaching approach

  • Student-led decisions with coach guidance
  • Emphasis on Core Values and gracious professionalism
  • Balanced focus: missions, design, and judging

Logistics & Enrollment

  • Small teams; weekly sessions
  • Parents receive progress notes
  • Equipment provided in-session

Interested in a team or starting a new one? Reach out and we’ll share current availability and next steps.
